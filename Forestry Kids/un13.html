FORESTRY KIDS

Climate change is a real and unquestionable threat to our world.

The effects are already visible and will be disastrous unless we act now. Through education, invention of new things and striving to achieve climate goals, we can make the necessary changes to protect the planet. These changes also provide huge opportunities to make our current parts of our world more innovative, which will create new jobs and help with equality across the globe. One main factor of this is the deforestation of large forests in our world.
